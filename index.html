AnimX Quick Guide

×

1. DRAW (Drawing & Layers)

  • Tools: Use the G-Pen for clean, pressure-sensitive lines, or the Shadow tool for watercolor-like blending.
  • 3D Reference: Toggle the 3D reference and adjust Yaw/Pitch to guide your character's angles.
  • Layers: Each layer can act as a single frame for your animation. Add, duplicate, or reorder them in the right panel.

2. ANIMATION (Animation Sequence)

  • Switch to the ANIMATION tab to start building your animation timeline.
  • Click on a layer thumbnail to append it to your Sequence.
  • Drag & Drop items in the Sequence list to reorder them, or use the Copy/Del buttons.
  • Adjust the FPS (speed) and hit Play to preview your work.

3. EXPORT (Save & Share)

  • Go to the IMPORT/EXPORT tab when you are ready.
  • Export MP4 (iPad): The most reliable format for iOS and social media sharing.
  • Export .animx: Saves your entire project (layers, sequences, and settings) so you can import and edit it later.
